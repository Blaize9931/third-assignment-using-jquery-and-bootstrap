Dragapult/Dusknoir deck

This is a brief introduction into the pokemon trading card game deck Dragapult

It is a simple deck and with the right pilot it can easily overcome most decks in the format. This page will briefly explain why.

Dragapult

Main role is to spread damage counters onto the bench, along with phantom dive dealing 200 damage to the active pokemon.

Budew

Main role is to stop opponent using item cards, slowing them down.

Dusknoir/Dusclops

Main role is blow itself up. In return it will place damage counters on any pokemon. It will also force opponent to take prize card.

Stadiums

Stadium cards: These cards have effects that change the state of the game. Put a condution on the game, you could say. The stadium of chose for this deck is the Jamming tower, as we do not run any tool cards, and this will shut off any tool card an enemy would like to play.

Supporters

Supporters: a player can activate one per turn. The provided picture is of the Iono supporter. This is especially powerful partnered with budew(the green thing sliding over the screen in the carousel.) The objective of this card would be to draw more cards, or if the game is nearing the final stages you'd be able to use this card to force your opponent to a very small hand. This will make it less likely they will have the card/squence they require to end the game.

Items

Item cards: This type of card can be played as many times as wanted within a single turn. However a single card has a 1 use expiry. The example above is of the Counter catcher! A impressive card, that paired with Dusknoir(the purple thing sliding on the carousel) can be a pretty mean beast. In short, dusknoir will blow up, forcing your opponent to take a prize card, putting them ahead. This will enable counter catcher. With CC you will switch thhe opponents active pokemon, and be able to kill it.
